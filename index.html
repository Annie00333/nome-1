Welcome to Heldorya

Step into a land of magic and valor, where heroes forge their legends within the walls of the mighty Castello di Valdareth. Here, your journey begins at the grand lobby, a beacon for all adventurers seeking glory and camaraderie. Join us in this rich medieval realm filled with wonders, challenges, and endless adventure.

Information for Brave Players

Heldorya is a modded Minecraft server running Forge 1.20.1, offering a balanced blend of classic medieval style and modern gameplay enhancements. To enter, kindly contact our admins as the modpack is exclusive. Immerse yourself in vast biomes, engaging quests, and unique player-driven commerce.

Laws of the Land

  • No griefing or destroying player builds.
  • Respect all players and admins; harassment is strictly forbidden.
  • Use of cheats or hacks results in immediate ban.
  • Keep language respectful and appropriate.
  • Trading must be fair and honest.
  • Do not exploit bugs or glitches.
  • Follow event and admin instructions without argument.

Explore the World

From the soaring towers of Valdareth to the bustling market square, every corner of Heldorya tells a story. Build your own homestead in the village—only those who purchase a house can call the spawn their home. Venture through the marketplace to find rare goods, or visit the sacred church for blessings on your quests.

Protection & Justice

In Heldorya, justice is swift and firm. Players who cause damage to others or property may be confined within the castle’s underground cells to atone for their misdeeds. Respect and order are the pillars of our realm.
